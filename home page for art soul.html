Ancient art refers to the many types of art produced by the advanced cultures of ancient societies with some form of writing,
such as those of ancient China, India, Mesopotamia, Persia, Palestine, Egypt, Greece, and Rome. The art of pre-literate societies
is normally referred to as Prehistoric art.
